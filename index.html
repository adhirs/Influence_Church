Our Beliefs

  • We believe in God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit: three persons existing as one being having equal power and authority
  • We believe in the written Word of God, the Bible
  • We believe in all the gifts of the Holy Spirit as stated in the Bible

Our Purpose

  • To influence individuals into a relationship with God
  • To raise up Individuals of Influence
  • To influence the current and future generations
  • To influence culture, community and country

Our Place

  • A place for the broken, hurt and scarred
  • A place to find Purpose
  • A place to fulfil Destiny
  • A place to meet God
